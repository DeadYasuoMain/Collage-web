FAQ

Q1. What is the admission process? +

Admission is based on PPT and PET entrance examination and merit list.

Q2. What is the fee structure? +

The fee structure is as follows:
• ₹7,770 for Unreserved (General) category students.
• ₹2,770 for ST, SC, and OBC students with family income below ₹1,00,000.

Q3. How to get scholarship and what are its provisions? +

Scholarships are available based on merit and financial need. Apply through student portal.
